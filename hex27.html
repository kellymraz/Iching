27. THE CORNERS OF THE MOUTH (PROVIDING NOURISHMENT)

Give proper nourishment to yourself and others

The image of this hexagram is that of an open mouth. It comes to remind us that the nourishement of our bodies and spirits is important and merits our conscientious attention. The I Ching teaches us that if we with to gauge someone's character, we should notice what he nourishes in himself and in others... The first counsel is that we should not focus our minds on desire... The final counsel is that we observe tranquility in speech, thought, and action.
